Best application for connecting with professional people worldwide. "Add skills" is also a good feature in the experience section. One feature I would love to see added is - in the "Edit Post" feature, users should also be able to add images which they might have missed adding initially. Bharat Khanna

I liked the LinkedIn app however from past couple of weeks the jobs section isn't loading anymore(at least in Android mobile app it isn't). Whenever I try clicking on "show all" jobs it doesn't redirect and just stays on the same page. Tried uninstalling and reinstalling the app but to no use. Alok Shukla

LinkedIn as a social media platform for making professional connections is really good. However, the app performance is not up to the mark. Links to profiles, searches within the app almost always show "page doesn't exist", "it wasn't you, it's us" in the first go. This needs to be fixed and the app should be optimised to improve user experience.

Furquan Saiyed

LinkedIn is a valuable professional networking platform that facilitates connections, job searches, and industry updates. It's user-friendly, offers a robust job board, and serves as an effective tool for personal branding. However, occasional irrelevant content and connection requests can be drawbacks.
